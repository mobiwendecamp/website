## A social and fair mobility transition

After the IAA took over Munich's city center in 2021 and 2023 and we already made a statement against this car-centered and antisocial mobility transition with our camps, we are also starting in 2025 with a camp for everyone.

## For everyone

This year we want to put a special focus on welcoming the entire society of citizens, activists and democrats to our camp. So be excited about what awaits you in Luipoldpark in 2025, come to our organization and help us organize a camp for everyone!

> No further program proposals will be accepted. Look forward to an exciting program, which will be available at <https://www.mobiwende.camp/program> soon!

We need your help! 

The camp will start in less than three weeks. We still need lots of support with setup, takedown, and of course during the camp itself. We’re happy about every bit of support! [Sign up here now.](https://mobiwende.camp/camp/participate#participate)

We’re looking for people for the following tasks during the camp:

🥤 Drinks Stand
Sit at the beverage stand, hand out drinks & collect donations.

👧 Childcare
Pavilion with crafts & games (ages 4–12).
👉 Support from an experienced educator.

🕯️ Vigil
Be present at the subway station, show visibility, report police harassment.

🧹 Cleaning Coordinator
Coordinate cleaning shifts.
👉 Organize people, explain the job & check supplies.

🎤 Program Support / Artist Care
Contact person for speakers & musicians.
👉 Pick up, accompany, help with questions & tech needs.

ℹ️ Camp Info
Daytime at the info desk.
👉 Answer questions, maintain shift schedules, be the go-to point for the camp.

👮‍♀️ Assembly Lead / Security Night Watch
8h Assembly Lead = be reachable on site, contact with police & authorities.
5h Night Watch Lead = coordinate & be present.
👉 Usually relaxed, experience helpful but not required.

⚡️ Tech Support
On call for power, sanitation & stage. If you can already join setup to get to know everything, that’s great.
👉 Once things are running, little to do. Prior knowledge is nice but not a must.

Thanks so much for your support! Please share this post widely – we need every one of you ❤️

## We need you!

The camp is organized, planned and set up by a number of volunteers. Do you want to help us and also get involved in a fair mobility transition? Here you can find out how:

## Plan, organize and meet new people

Would you like to get fully involved in the organization work, help design, plan and support the camp? Then come to our organization. We offer onboardings at regular intervals, where you can find out how you can help and also ask questions that may still be unanswered. The next onboarding is on **11.03.25 18:00.** Just write us an email at **mitmachen@mobiwende.camp**.

## Help on site

You want to support the camp, but all the organizational work is too much for you and you would rather help directly at the camp? No problem. We always need hard-working hands to help us set up and dismantle, as well as to take on various tasks during the camp. We will soon put a helper tool online that you can sign up for and we will then get in touch with you. Until then, you can sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media so you don't miss anything.

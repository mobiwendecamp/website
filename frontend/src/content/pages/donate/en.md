### Cash donations:

This year we have a lot of expenses related to the camp again. From tents to toilets to electricity, we have a lot of rental costs. We try to organize as much as possible inexpensively or free of charge, but that doesn't work for everything. We also try to finance the camp with applications to foundations or similar, but we rely on donations for complete financing.

We have started a crowdfunding campaign!

Half of the mayor's limousine - that would be enough to finance our camp for 1500 people.
So actually a bargain! Unfortunately, we don't have access to Dieter Reiter's luxury car, so we need you, because we have to pay a lot of bills before the camp even starts.

You can donate here: <https://www.betterplace.org/de/projects/151831-mobilitaetswendecamp-2025>

Please spread the word about our crowdfunding campaign!

Thank you! 🙂

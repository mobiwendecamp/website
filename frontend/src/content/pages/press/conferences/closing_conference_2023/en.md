From September 5th to September 10th, the International Automobile Exhibition took place in Munich for the second time.

Activists set up a protest camp in Luitpoldpark. Activists from a wide spectrum gathered there to protest against the destruction of the environment by motor vehicles and their contribution to the climate catastrophe.

The day after the end of the IAA 2023, representatives from various groups drew a conclusion about the protest actions.

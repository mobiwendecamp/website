## Give us a hand

### Help with set-up and dismantling

Tents, water/sewage, electricity, seating, sun protection, etc. need to be set up and dismantled. You can learn lots of cool new skills about logistics and infrastructure and at the end of the construction you can be happy that everyone has water and a roof over their heads. Construction begins on **05.09.25** and continues until the start of the camp on **09.09.25**. Dismantling is from **15.09.25** until **17.09.25**.

### Tasks during camp

There's a whole array of tasks that we need to schedule before the camp: tech maintenance, childcare, night-watch leader, service desk, and many more. You'll receive a detailed instruction and can always reach a responsible person for questions. Even three hours are invaluable. You'll learn a lot and probably have fun!

## Become a part of the camp

It's never too late to join our little group of organizers. If you want to join help and support, write to us at [mitmachen@mobiwende.camp](mailto:mitmachen@mobiwende.camp) or just go to the info tent at the camp itself. See you!

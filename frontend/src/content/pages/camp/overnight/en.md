## Sleeping

At our camp there will be large areas where you can set up tents you have brought with you. Areas A-G are designated on our tent areas. Certain areas of these are reserved for [Safer Spaces](https://mobiwende.camp/glossar#safer-space).

When you arrive at the camp, please ask at the info point about the layout of the tent area.

## Sanitary facilities

We offer toilets and showers at the camp, which can be used free of charge.

## Injuries and infection protection

There is a comprehensive hygiene concept that covers infectious diseases and a sani crew that can take care of possible injuries.
